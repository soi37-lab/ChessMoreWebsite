Our Mission

At ChessMore Africa, our mission is to promote chess as a tool for educational development, fostering critical thinking, and building a strong community of players. We aim to nurture talent and inspire the next generation of leaders.

Non-Discrimination Policy

ChessMore Africa is committed to providing a safe, inclusive environment for all individuals, regardless of race, gender, age, religion, disability, or background. We believe in the power of diversity to strengthen our community.

Policy on Relationship with Minors

We prioritize the safety and well-being of minors. Our staff and members are required to adhere to strict guidelines to ensure respectful, appropriate interactions. Protecting children is a core value of ChessMore Africa.
